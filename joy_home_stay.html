1. Transportation:
Transportation services are available specifically for students during college timings.

2. Water Facility:
Both hot and cold water options are provided to cater to your personal preferences.

3. Weekly Food Menu:
The hostel provides a weekly food menu that can be accessed through the provided portal.

4. Security Amount:
A fixed security amount of 12000 rupees is required upon admission. This amount is refundable at the end of the session.

5. Hostel Timings:
The hostel premises adhere to a time restriction from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM for all students staying in the hostel.

6. Electricity Services:
Electricity usage up to 100 units is free of charge. However, any excess units consumed will incur additional charges imposed by the hostel.

7. Laundry Conditions:
Each student is allowed up to 10 kg of laundry per service.

8. Additional Benefits:
To enhance your stay, the hostel offers additional services such as a gym, garden, CCTV surveillance, power backup, and common area providing added comfort and convenience.
